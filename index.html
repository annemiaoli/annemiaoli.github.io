about


Whether I'm sprinting through the streets of the city or sipping chai at my favorite coffeeshop, my audio recorder is at my side. My love for stories has introduced me to the world through the ears of a woman exploring living and a reporter exploring life.


I am a student at the Medill School at Northwestern University. I like making the little stories big (contact me and I'll explain what I mean!) I am particularly interested in topics pertaining to education, class, race and China.


In my free time, I code or procrastinate on Humans of New York and Wikipedia, or by re-reading a good book.
